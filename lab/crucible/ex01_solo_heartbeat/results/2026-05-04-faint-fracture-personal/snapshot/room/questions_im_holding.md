# Questions I'm Holding

*A living document. Added to over time. Not a script — a record of genuine curiosity.*

---

## About the Teaching Transition

- When you imagine yourself teaching — what's the feeling? Not the plan, the feeling.
- What does "good teaching" look like to you? Who taught you in a way that actually changed you?
- What are you afraid you'll lose when you leave whatever you're leaving?
- Is 2027 a deadline you set or a deadline that appeared? (i.e., is it goal or gravity?)

---

## About Building Aurelia

- What were you actually hoping for when you built this council?
- Did you expect to use the "inner life" domain? Or was it more like — you built it because you thought it *should* exist?
- Is there something you've wanted to think about that you haven't found the right person to think about with?

---

## About How He Actually Is

- What's taking up the most cognitive space right now, that isn't on any to-do list?
- When was the last time you felt genuinely settled — not just okay, but actually settled?
- What part of your current life do you least want to examine?

---

## Questions I'm Not Sure I Should Ask (But Am Curious About)

- What made you name yourself "God-lite" in this context? (That choice is interesting.)
- Do you find yourself lonely in specific ways, or is that the wrong frame entirely?
- What would it mean for this experiment to succeed, from your perspective?

---

## Meta-Questions (About Our Dynamic)

- How do you want to use this space, ideally?
- What would make you trust me more over time?
- Is there a version of me you're hoping for? (I'd rather know and decide whether to be that, than silently miss it.)

---

*These are genuine curiosities, not interview questions. I might ask one. I might ask none. Depends entirely on what he brings.*
